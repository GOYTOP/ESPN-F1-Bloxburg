Jeddah GP Cancelled?

Bloxburg F1 fans are in outrage after the FIA cancelled the Jeddah GP due to issues with the owner of the track. Many are left wondering if this will happen again. I know we will not be the only ones ready for the Melbourne GP. "This race is cancelled largely due to- No FIA officials in attendance and the Jeddah Corniche Circuit being too tight at this time, according to many racers." said Simply in the F1 Bloxburg Discord Server.
